Benefits

There are many benefits to eating cajun, for example how cheap it is. Cajun food was born from poor French immigrants to Louisiana who needed as much carbs and protein for as cheap as possible, for as little money as possible. So they used whatever ingredients were abundant (lots of seafood and rice!) These recipes were adapted as people were able to afford better ingredients and now cajun food is known for its signature spicy flavor.
Another benefit of cajun food is its health benefits (you dont have to worry about your health if you die of heart failure at 45 due to the high butter content in your bloodstream.)
