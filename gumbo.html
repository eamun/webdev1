Gumbo recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 3/4 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 2 chopped onions (white or yellow)
  • 450g smoked sausage
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 3-4 cups hot water
  • 2 cups chopped oysters
  • 1/2 teaspoon gumbo filé (replace with okra or celery salt if you dont have this)

Procedure

  1. 1. Salt and pepper chicken pieces and set aside.
  2. 2. In a large, heavy pot combine flour and oil.
  3. 3. Cook on medium heat until dark brown, stirring constantly.
  4. 4. Add onions and cook until soft.
  5. 5. Place chicken pieces in the pot and cover.
  6. 6. Smother on medium heat for 30 minutes, stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  7. 7. Stir in sausage and parsley and smother for 10 more minutes.
  8. 8. Pour in enough hot water to cover chicken, and simmer until tender.
  9. 9. Remove from heat and skim off excess grease.
  10. Optionally
    • • Fry the smoked sausage beforehand to get rid of some of the fats and add texture.
    • • Add cayenne or sliced chillis if you want it spicy.
    • • Serve over rice and with potato salad is very popular.

About Gumbo

Gumbo is a soup/stew of creole cuisine and has African, American and European characteristics. It was adapted by the immagrants to Louisiana from the native americans. My parents would always make it when the rest of the family would come over as it's very good for feeding a large group of people. It's also a good recipe to get rid of perishible meats and vegetable as it keeps very well and even tastes better the day after, just do not store with the rice mixed in.
